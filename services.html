Our Chiropractic Services

Comprehensive, family-focused chiropractic care for every stage of life — from pregnancy to pediatrics to lifelong wellness in Madison, AL.

Core Service

Chiropractic Care

Chiropractic care is the foundation of what we do at New Life Family Chiropractic. Dr. Misty Browning uses gentle, specific spinal adjustments to correct vertebral subluxations — areas where the spine has shifted out of alignment and is interfering with your nervous system. When these subluxations are removed, your brain and body can communicate clearly again, allowing your body to function at its highest potential and heal itself naturally.

Many patients in Madison and the surrounding North Alabama communities come to us for relief from back pain, neck pain, headaches, sciatica, and more. But regular chiropractic care goes far beyond pain relief — it is a powerful, preventive form of healthcare that supports your overall wellness for a lifetime.

  • Whole-person assessment
  • Gentle, specific spinal adjustments
  • Personalized care plans
  • Suitable for all ages
Movement & Strength

Corrective Exercises

Corrective exercises are an important complement to chiropractic adjustments. While adjustments restore proper alignment and motion to the spine, the muscles surrounding the spine must also be retrained to support those corrections. Without this, the body may simply return to its old, dysfunctional patterns.

Dr. Browning will prescribe specific, simple exercises you can do at home to strengthen weak muscles, stretch tight ones, and stabilize your corrections. Over time these movements help your adjustments "hold" longer and lead to lasting improvements in posture, mobility, and overall function.

  • Customized at-home exercise plans
  • Postural correction movements
  • Core stabilization work
  • Long-term spinal support

For Little Ones

Pediatric Chiropractic

Children experience rapid growth and countless physical stresses — from the birth process itself to playground falls, sports, and heavy backpacks. Pediatric chiropractic care supports healthy development by keeping the spine and nervous system functioning optimally during these crucial years.

Dr. Browning is ICPA (International Chiropractic Pediatric Association) trained, equipping her to make gentle, specific, and scientific adjustments appropriate for your child's developing spine. Adjustments for children use very light pressure — often no more than you would use to test the ripeness of a tomato — and are safe, gentle, and effective.

  • ICPA trained pediatric chiropractor
  • Gentle, age-appropriate adjustments
  • Supports healthy development
  • Helps with colic, ear infections, sleep & more
Webster Certified

Pre & Postnatal Chiropractic

Pregnancy places unique demands on a mother's body. As the belly grows, posture shifts, the pelvis widens, and hormones loosen ligaments throughout the body — all of which can lead to misalignments, low back pain, sciatica, and pelvic discomfort.

Dr. Browning is a Webster Certified chiropractor. The Webster Technique is a specific, scientific analysis and adjustment of the sacrum and pelvis used to decrease symptoms caused by sacral subluxations during pregnancy. By balancing the pelvis and reducing tension in the surrounding ligaments and muscles, we help create a more comfortable pregnancy and an environment in which the baby can move into the best possible position for birth.

Postnatal care supports the body's recovery, helps with postural changes from nursing and carrying baby, and gently restores pelvic and spinal balance.

  • Webster Certified Chiropractor
  • Safe throughout all trimesters
  • Pelvic balance & alignment
  • Comfortable pregnancy & faster postpartum recovery
