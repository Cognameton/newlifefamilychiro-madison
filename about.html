Meet The Doctor

Dr. Misty Browning, DC

Webster Certified ICPA Trained 20+ Years Experience Parker College Graduate

Dr. Misty Browning is a Madison, AL chiropractor who serves the Madison community and the surrounding North Alabama area. She uses Chiropractic care to improve the health and wellness in all areas of her patients' lives.

Dr. Browning takes a "whole person" approach in Chiropractic care, which means looking for the underlying causes of disease, discomfort, and pain, as opposed to just treating the symptoms. Many seemingly unrelated symptoms often arise from imbalances in the spinal column, and Dr. Browning will determine the root of the pain and create a personalized Chiropractic and wellness plan to suit each patient's individual needs.

Her Chiropractic Philosophy

Under the supervision of a caring and skilled chiropractor, patients report higher functioning in all areas of their lives. By adjusting vertebral subluxations that block your brain and body from being able to communicate effectively, your body can function to its highest potential.

Specialized Training & Expertise

  • Webster Certified Chiropractor – Uses a specific, scientific technique to help decrease symptoms caused by sacral subluxations during pregnancy.
  • ICPA (International Chiropractic Pediatric Association) Trained – Equipping her to make gentle, specific, and scientific adjustments to your child's spine based on their needs.
  • Pregnancy & Pediatric Specialist – Passionate about equipping moms with all the tools needed as the body prepares for birth.
  • Family Wellness Care – Provides ongoing wellness care for the entire family.

Our Mission

Our mission at New Life Family Chiropractic is simply to serve you. We provide excellent chiropractic care to help our patients reach and exceed their health goals. We assess the patient as a whole, address the main concern while leading patients into a lifestyle of being healthy.
